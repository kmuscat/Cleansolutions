A LONG HISTORY FROM

HUMBLE BEGINNINGS

With a long history stemming back over three generations, the Stehle family has become a leader in mobile wash solutions. After Windsor native Mike Stehle started his mobile car washing business back in 60's, he quickly expanded to service the greater Windsor area. Specializing in large commercial trucking fleets, he then brought his two sons - Mark and Vince into the family business.

After competing on the National stage as one of Canada's prominent junior cyclists, Vince decided to settle in Barrie and start a family. He then incorporated Clean Solutions Ltd. in 1997, quickly growing to become Barrie's go-to mobile wash services provider for both the commercial trucking and automotive industry. Following a rapid expansion Vince then grew the company to provide municipal grafiiti removal and custom cleaning services for a broad range of clients across every industry.

Now in their 21st year, the company has been passed along to Vince's son Eddy. Having worked alongside his father for over a decade, Eddy has recently become president with his father remaining chairholder. With a focus on expanding the companie's reach to include a greater territory, Eddy has slowly begun adding trucks and undertaking larger contracts.
